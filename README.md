# Spelunk Vault for Nonfiction Book Development

The Spelunk Vault system is designed to manage nonfiction book projects, from early research and outlining through manuscript completion. It handles:
- Book structure and outline development
- Research and source materials
- Manuscript drafting and revision
- Team coordination
- Reference management

The file/folder system is designed for [Obsidian.md](https://obsidian.md/) and [Massive Wiki](https://massive.wiki/). For multi-device or teams, you can use Obsidian Sync (paid, easy) or Git (free, a little technical).

Spelunk Vault also works well with [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/claude-code) for AI-assisted research, outlining, and drafting — alongside Obsidian for everyday writing and navigation. This workflow is covered in Peter Kaminski's course, ["Agentic AI with Pete"](https://learn.peterkaminski.ai/).

## Related Frameworks

Spelunk Vault is a sibling framework to [Arc Vault](https://github.com/peterkaminski/arc-vault) and [Prisma Vault](https://github.com/peterkaminski/prisma-vault), adapted for nonfiction book writing:

- **Prisma Vault**: Designed for serialized fiction with episodes and seasons
- **Arc Vault**: Designed for multi-volume novels with continuous narrative arcs
- **Spelunk Vault**: Designed for nonfiction book projects
