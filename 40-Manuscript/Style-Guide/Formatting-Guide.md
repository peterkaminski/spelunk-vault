## Overview

Formatting conventions for the manuscript files in Obsidian and for export to publisher-ready formats.

## Headings

- Use `#` for chapter title only
- Use `##` for major sections within a chapter
- Use `###` for subsections
- Avoid going deeper than `###` in manuscript files

## Emphasis

- *Italics* for titles of works, foreign words, and emphasis
- **Bold** for key terms on first introduction only
- Avoid underlining
