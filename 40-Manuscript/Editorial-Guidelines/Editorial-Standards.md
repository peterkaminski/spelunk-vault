## Overview

Editorial standards and expectations for the manuscript review process.

## Review Stages

1. **Developmental edit:** Big-picture feedback on structure, argument, pacing, and completeness
2. **Line edit:** Sentence-level improvements to clarity, flow, and style
3. **Copy edit:** Grammar, punctuation, consistency, fact-checking
4. **Proofread:** Final pass for typos and formatting errors
